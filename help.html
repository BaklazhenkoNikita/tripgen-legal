Help Center

Everything you need to get the most out of TripGen.

Getting Started

How do I create a trip?

Tap the AI chat tab and describe where you want to go and what you're interested in. TripGen's AI will generate a personalized itinerary for you. You can also use the guided trip creator for a step-by-step experience.

Do I need an account?

You can explore and use TripGen as a guest. Creating an account lets you save trips, sync across devices, and get more personalized recommendations.

How does the AI know what I like?

During onboarding, you pick your travel style and interests. As you use the app - swiping on activities, saving places, and creating trips - TripGen learns your preferences to give better recommendations over time.

Discover and Explore

What is the Swipe feature?

Swipe right on activities you like and left on ones you don't. This helps TripGen understand your taste and suggest better activities for your trips.

Why does TripGen need my location?

Location access lets us show activities and points of interest near you. It's optional - you can always search for any city manually. You can change location permissions anytime in your device settings.

Managing Trips

Can I edit my itinerary?

Yes! After generating a trip, you can add or remove activities, rearrange days, and customize your schedule. Just open any trip and make changes directly.

How do trip reminders work?

When you set a start date for your trip, TripGen can send you a reminder the day before departure and a morning brief on your first day. You'll be asked to enable notifications when you set your first trip date.

Tip: Notifications respect quiet hours (10 PM - 8 AM) and are limited to avoid being intrusive.

Booking

Can I book activities through TripGen?

TripGen provides links to booking platforms where you can reserve activities, tours, and accommodations. Tapping a booking link will open the provider's website in your browser.

Account and Data

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings and tap "Delete Account". This will permanently remove your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We use encrypted connections (HTTPS) for all data transfers and store your authentication tokens securely on your device. We don't sell your data to third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Troubleshooting

The app isn't loading or seems slow

TripGen requires an internet connection for AI features. Check your connection and try again. If the issue persists, try closing and reopening the app.

I'm not getting notifications

Make sure notifications are enabled in your device Settings > TripGen > Notifications. Also check that you've set a start date for your trip - reminders are only sent for trips with dates.
