Planning

Plan by vibe, not by checklist.

Describe the trip you want — relaxed, foodie, active, cultural — and who's coming along. Periplo shapes pacing, neighborhood mix, and activity density to match. Nothing generic.

No sign-up wall, no survey fatigue. A few taps and you're planning.

Itinerary

An itinerary, not a list.

Day by day, hour by hour. Landmarks, restaurants, and hidden spots blended with realistic pacing — so you're not sprinting between stops or killing time between reservations.

Reorganize a day, swap an activity, or regenerate just one block. The rest adjusts around it.

On the ground

Built for walking, not for tabs.

Every day gets a map with a clean route between stops. Neighborhoods stay grouped. Distances, opening windows, and travel times come built in — so the plan survives contact with the sidewalk.

Context

Context for every pin.

Hours, price, typical duration, distance from your base, address, and a local tip. Not boilerplate — specific notes about when to go, what to skip, and what's easy to miss.

FAQ

The short version.

Do I need an account?

No. Periplo works in guest mode — everything local on your device. Sign in later if you want to sync across devices or collaborate.

Which cities are supported?

Any city you can name. Periplo isn't a curated list — it plans trips to anywhere with enough information online. Tokyo, Lisbon, Oaxaca, or your neighbor over.

Will the itinerary actually be accurate?

AI-generated, so always confirm opening hours, ticket availability, and reservations before you go. Periplo links to booking sites for anything that matters, and tells you when tickets sell out fast. See our AI disclosure.

Can I use it offline?

Yes. Download a trip and the full itinerary — maps, activity details, restaurants — works without signal. Perfect for the subway and for places with spotty data.

How do I cancel Pro?

iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Periplo → Cancel. You keep Pro until the end of the billing period. No refund questions, no friction.

Is my data sold or shared?

Never. No advertising identifiers, no cross-site tracking, no data sales. See the full privacy policy.
